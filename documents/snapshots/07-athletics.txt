George Mason Student Ticket Information

Free Admission Policy
Currently enrolled students at George Mason University are provided FREE admission to ALL George Mason athletic events.
Note: Complimentary admission may not be available to students for post-season events or non-Mason events hosted on campus.

How to Claim Free Tickets
Students must use the Mason360 system. Tickets can be claimed with these simple steps:
1) Open Mason360 App
2) Select Events Tab from bottom
3) Select Filter Option in Top Right
4) Filter by group, "Mason Athletics"
5) Select on the game you wish to get your ticket
6) Select "Register"
7) Hit the back arrow and click on "1 ticket" to get your ticket QR code

QR Code Requirements
NOTE: QR Code WILL BE required for entry. No paper tickets will be accepted. EagleBank Arena or George Mason Athletics staff can deny anyone trying to enter with a paper QR Code.

This system replaces the old Mason Patriots ticket app and aims to make the ticket process simpler and more efficient for Mason students.
